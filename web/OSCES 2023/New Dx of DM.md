## Management:
1. First line = lifestyle advice
2. Second line - metformin to increase insulin sensitivity
	1. Can cause GI issues, renal issues
3. Can add on SU, GLP-1 (-glutide), GPP4 (-gliptin)
	4. Be careful of hypos! cannot use in pancreatitis 
4. Consider SGLT2 inhibitor if heart failure 

## Safety net - sudden fatigue/syncope, come to ED (might be hypoglycaemic or DKA)!
